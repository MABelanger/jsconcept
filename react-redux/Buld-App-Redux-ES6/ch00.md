## Why Redux
It become the most popular data management library.
* All state is centralize into one Store
* Reduced Boilerplate
* Isomorphic
* Immutable Store
* Hot Reloading
* Time-travel debugging
* Small (2k)

Redux is build on Flux with unidirectional data flow.
